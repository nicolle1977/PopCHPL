National NAGPRA Program

This is a link to the National NAGPRA Program home page, headed by the National Park Service. You can links to applying for NAGPRA grants, sign up for their newsletter and training, as well as their list of grant recipients. (Image Courtesy NPS NAGPRA)

National Association of Tribal Historic Preservation Officers

A resource for finding tribal historic preservation officers to partner with in repatriations. You can sign up for their newsletter, find out about their current projects and get a list of their conferences. (Image Courtesy National Association of Tribal Historic Preservation Officers)

National Congress of American Indians

The National Congress of American Indians is the oldest, largest and most representative American Indian and Alaska Native organization serving the broad interests of tribal governments and communities.

Contact the NPS

This is a direct link to the contact page for the NPS containing their mailing address, their leadership structure and program officers.

MACPRA

MACPRA is the Michigan Anishinaabek Cultural Preservation & Repatriation Alliance encompassing two of the state recognized tribes and all of the federally recognized tribes withing the State of Michigan.

American Alliance of Museums

Here you can find information about curatorial standards used throughout the United States. The AAM is the certifying agency in the US for curatorial standards.
