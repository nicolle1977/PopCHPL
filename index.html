Welcome to Protecting Our Past, an Introduction to Cultural Heritage Policy and Law


The course includes:

  • NAGPRA
  • NHPA
  • UNESCO
  • Case studies for each policy
  • Sources and links about each policy
  • Information about the Instructor
  • A sharing section

Each policy page includes the various parts of the laws, case studies for each policy, and links for further information. The Instructor page offers a background of creator of this project and links to some of the communities they have worked with. Our sharing section includes a place to discuss mitigations projects, how they started and how they were handeled and a space for comments and suggestions.
